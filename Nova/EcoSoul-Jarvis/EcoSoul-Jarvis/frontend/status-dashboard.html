↓

↓

↓

↓
Products stored in window.allHomeProducts
↓

↓
Event listeners attach to search/filter inputs
↓
User types in search box
↓
filterHomeProducts() runs instantly
↓
Grid updates with filtered results
↓
User clicks "Add to Cart"
↓
Product added to shopping cart (if logged in)
